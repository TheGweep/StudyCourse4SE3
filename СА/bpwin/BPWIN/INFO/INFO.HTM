BPwin

Current Version: 2.5
First Release: 1994
Latest Release: August 1998
 
Operating System Compatibility:
Windows NT, Windows 95, Windows 98
 
Technical Requirements:
Minimum 10 MB hard disk space, 16 MB RAM
 
Product Focus:
Business process modeling, business process improvement, IDEF0 modeling, IDEF3 modeling, data flow diagramming
 
Description:
PLATINUM BPwin is a powerful modeling tool used to analyze, document, and improve complex business processes. For example, using a BPwin model you can clearly document which activities are needed, how they are performed, and what resources are required. This provides an integrated picture of how your organization gets things done — from small department workflow models to complex node tree diagrams. If you are building or purchasing software, business process models also serve as requirements documentation, helping to ensure that your IT investments fulfill business needs. BPwin models help you understand business processes and the impact of business events, and define how business processes interact with your data. Inefficient, wasteful or redundant activities can be easily detected — and subsequently improved, replaced or eliminated — to help you meet corporate objectives.
 
BPwin is scalable to support enterprise business models through its integration with PLATINUM ModelMart, a model management system. BPwin process models can be synchronized with PLATINUM ERwin data models, allowing you to verify that your information resources optimally support your business processes.
 
Benefits:

  • Saves time and money by analyzing how change will affect the business, then determining the best solution, and mapping the solution to existing information systems.
  • Assists organizations with altering business strategies, allowing them to take a picture of where the business is today and map it to a picture of where the business needs to be.
  • BPwin models provide the framework for understanding a business's processes, determining the impact of business events and defining how the processes interact with the data flowing through the organization.
  • Supports multiple modeling techniques in one tool, integrating three key business perspectives to serve the needs of business and technology users.
  • Verifies that information architectures support business processes through integration with PLATINUM ERwin.
  • Provides workgroup and enterprise-wide model collaboration through integration with PLATINUM ModelMart.
